About

Hello, I'm Kübra Buzlu, a third-year student at Marmara University in Istanbul. Ever since I was introduced to the world of Artificial Intelligence, I have been fascinated by its capabilities and its potential to change the way we live our lives. Specifically, I have a keen interest in Machine Learning, Artificial Neural Networks, Image Processing, and Natural Language Processing.

One of the most exciting projects I've had the opportunity to work on so far was a TÜBİTAK-funded project called "Turkmed: Original Machine Learning and Deep Learning Methods Including Diffusion and Seq2seq Fusion Algorithms to Overcome Ambiguity in Word Meaning and Classify Turkish Texts by Theme/Emotion." I was happy to be worked as a project scholarship holder under the guidance of Berna Altınel, one of the faculty members at Marmara University. During the project, I gained valuable experience in Natural Language Processing, and I was thrilled to have contributed to the development of an academic article based on our findings. You can find the related publication in the academic publications section below.

While I enjoyed working on Natural Language Processing, I am currently more focused on Image Processing. For this purpose, I completed a summer internship at Innova Information Technologies, where I worked on Image Processing. I am continuing my research on Image Processing in the medical field as an R&D assistant specialist at Innova Information Technologies, where I use Artificial Neural Networks and Image Processing techniques to process medical images.

I believe that Artificial Intelligence has the power to change the world, and I am excited to be part of this rapidly evolving field. My dedication to Artificial Intelligence technologies and my research experiences have helped me develop the skills I need to become a successful professional in the field of Image Processing and Natural Language Processing.
